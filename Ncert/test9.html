CBSE Sample Papers

CBSE Sample Papers for 2021-22 Examinations are released by the board on 2nd September 2021. These papers are for the Term 1 examinations which are to be conducted in the month of November-December 2021. These CBSE Sample paper are based on the latest exam pattern. Students can download the CBSE Sample Papers in PDF format or refer to them online for practice, as per their preference. CBSE Sample Papers are the best resource for students preparing for the board exam at home during this COVID-19 pandemic period. For the academic session 2021-22, CBSE has introduced the special assessment scheme for board Classes 10th and 12th due to the COVID 19 pandemic. As per this scheme, the academic session is divided into two terms with approximately 50% syllabus in each term. The Board will conduct examinations at the end of each term based on the bifurcated syllabus. The first term examination will be conducted between November-December 2021. The duration of the exam will be 90 minutes. The exam will be based on Multiple Choice Questions (MCQ) covering case-based MCQs and MCQs on assertion-reasoning. The Board has released the CBSE Sample Paper for the Term 1 examination. The latest CBSE Sample Paper 2021-22 for Term 1 Class 10 and 12 board examination are based on the revised and reduced CBSE Syllabus 2021-22. The Term 2 examination will be conducted on March-April 2022. The exam will be for 2 hours, and the paper will have questions of different formats (case-based/ situation based, open-ended- short answer/ long answer type). If the situation is not conducive for normal descriptive examination, then a 90 minute MCQ based exam will be conducted at the end of Term II. The Board will provide its complete detail along with the CBSE Sample Papers for Term 2 examination 2021-22 for Classes 10th and 12th after the successful conduct of the Term 1 exam.
